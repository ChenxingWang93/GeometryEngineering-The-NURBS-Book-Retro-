An nth-degree power basis curve is given by 一条 n 次曲线的幂基表示形式

C(u)=(x(u),y(u),z(u))=\sum^{n}_{i=0}a_{i}u^{i} 0<=u<=1 (1.5)

The

a_{i}=(x_{i},y_{i},z_{i})

are vectors, hence 为向量，因此

x(u)=\sum_{i=0}^{n}x_{i}u^{i}
y(u)=\sum_{i=0}^{n}y_{i}u^{i}
z(u)=\sum_{i=0}^{n}z_{i}u^{i}

In matrix form Eq.(1.5) 写成矩阵形式(1.5)为

C(u)= \begin{bmatrix} a_{0} & a_{1} &. . .& a_{n} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1\\ u\\ .\\ .\\ .\\ u^{n} \end{bmatrix}= [a_{i}]^{T}[u_{i}] (1.6)
